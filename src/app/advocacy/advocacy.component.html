Advocacy & Legal Interventions

Advocacy

ASEEM engages with Government Officials and Elected Representatives to make them aware of the community needs, to streamline scheme criteria and procedures and simplify scheme application process. We periodically generate reports and organize consultations with different stakeholders.

Legal Interventions

ASEEM engages with Government Officials and Elected Representatives to make them aware of the community

Our advocacy efforts are augmented through Public Interest Litigations (PILs). PILs provide access to courts common people for legal redress and thus resolve long pending issues which effect marginalized communities. PIL is an important instrument of social change and for maintaining the Rule of law and accelerating the balance between law and justice. PILs have helped us to:

  • Obtain Stay Orders on the Implementation of NPR exercise in Telangana State
  • To initiate action against police officials for excesses committed on helpless people during the COVID lock down.
  • To initiate action against the state government for indiscriminately cancelling 20 lakh White Ration Card, thus depriving the poor of subsidized rations.
