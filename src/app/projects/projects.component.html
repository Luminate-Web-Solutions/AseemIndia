Projects

Leadership Empowerment and Advocacy Development (LEAD)

ASEEM collaborated with like minded institutions like CWS, HMI to develop a 10 module course which exposes its trainees to different movements and development initiatives apart from imparting skills to trigger and sustain initiatives and movements in their own communities.

We promote value based and Ethical Social Leadership amongst these community leaders and help them to chalk out career paths in the social development sector

2 Stage LEAD Process:

LEAD is currently being implemented in Hyderabad. Volunteers of Mujaba Helping Foundation are undergoing training in organizing local community for accessing government schemes and programmes.

Introductory Workshops

With heads of Civil Society Organizations on challenges faced by the community at social, economical and political levels and the way forward to address them through Leadership Development. These workshops enable ASEEM to indentify partners for running the LEAD Course.

10 Module LEAD Course

Volunteers/workers of partner organisations will undergo a Comprehensive Social Action Course. Participants who successfully complete the LEAD Course will be awarded a certificate

Action Research and Social Audit

We undertake Social Audit of different development programmes of the government to document its components, structure, processes and procedures, implementation at various levels - successes, bottlenecks, outreach and impact. At the community level, LEAD participants are involved to study the outreach, impact and bottlenecks at the grass roots implementation level.

The reports generated through this process are used to generate awareness amongst the civil society organisations about the implementation of the programme and thus involve them in advocacy interventions for effective implementation.

Currently we are engaged in Social Audit of the Telangana State Minority Welfare Department.
