About Us

ASEEM engages in advocacy, legal interventions, action research, and empowerment of grassroots organizations and social workers in the Muslim community to enable marginalized communities to access their Constitutional Rights and Entitlements.

There are numerous Muslim charities that provide relief, succor, and support to millions of poor from within the community. However, a closer inspection reveals that their philanthropy creates a dependency that keeps the community distanced from the mainstream.
