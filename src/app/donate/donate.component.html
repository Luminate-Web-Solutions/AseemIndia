Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to ASEEM are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. We will provide you with a receipt that you can use for tax purposes.

How will my donation be used?

Your donation will be used to support our various programs, including facilitating access to welfare schemes, providing legal aid, conducting research, and advocating for policy changes that benefit marginalized communities.

Can I donate from outside India?

Currently, we can only accept donations from Indian bank accounts. We are working on setting up a system to accept international donations and will update our website once it's available.

Can I specify how my donation should be used?

Yes, you can specify if you want your donation to be used for a specific program or cause. Please mention this in the transaction note or email us after making the donation.
